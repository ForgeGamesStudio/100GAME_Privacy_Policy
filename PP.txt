Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-16

This application (“100GAME”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

Advertising Data
The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect and process certain information, including:
- Advertising ID
- IP address
- Device information such as device model and operating system version
- Approximate location derived from IP address
- Ad interaction and performance data

This data is used to display ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud and abuse.
Data collection and processing are governed by Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

App Usage Data
The App stores limited usage-related data locally on your device, such as:
- Whether you opened or dismissed a game offer
- Application settings (language, notification preferences, quiet hours)

This data:
- Is stored only on your device
- Is not transmitted to the developer’s servers
- Is not shared with third parties
- Is used solely to provide and improve app functionality

Network Requests
The App retrieves publicly available information about free game offers from:
- Steam
- Epic Games Store

No personal data is sent to these services. Network requests are used only to fetch publicly available content.

Data Sharing
The App does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties, except as necessary to provide advertising through Google AdMob.

Data Retention
The App does not operate its own backend servers and does not store personal data remotely.
All locally stored data remains on your device and can be removed at any time by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the App.

Your Choices
- You can disable notifications at any time through the App settings or system settings.
- You can clear all locally stored data via system settings.
- You can manage ad personalization through your Google account settings.

Children’s Privacy
The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

System Access Disclaimer
The App does not use background location, camera, microphone, or foreground services.

Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version of this document.
